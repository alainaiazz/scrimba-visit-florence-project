Top Three Activities To Do in Florence

Walk Around The Centre

The center of Florence has majestics arts, churches, museums adn culture specific shops and major fashion brands on its streets.

Visit Uffizzi

Visit the Uffizzi to access the collections of paintings from the 14th-century and Renaissance period, the major mastrpieces of that era.

Visit Chianti

Drive south of Florence to have a breath of air and experience the pinnacle of wine making, gorgeus soft hills and unique family owned wineries.
